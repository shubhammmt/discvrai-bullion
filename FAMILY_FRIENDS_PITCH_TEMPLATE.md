**Accent Colors:**

- **Headlines:** Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins)

- Modern, clean, minimal

- **Bar charts:** Comparisons
- **Tables:** Detailed data
- **Infographics:** Complex concepts

### Timing
- **Total:** 12-15 minutes

- **Slides 6-9:** 4 minutes (business model/traction)
- **Slides 10-14:** 4 minutes (team/financials/ask)

- **Q&A:** 10-15 minutes

### Key Messages
1. Problem is real: 95% of investors lack quality research
2. Solution is unique: AI + news + gamification + B2B
3. Market is huge: $50B+, growing 25% annually
4. Traction exists: MVP live, users growing
5. Economics work: Break-even in 11 months
6. Team can execute: Relevant experience, fully committed
7. Risk is low: Multiple revenue streams, conservative model
8. Timing is perfect: AI + retail boom converging now

### Storytelling Arc
1. Start with relatable problem (someone losing money)
2. Show vision (democratize professional research)
3. Prove it's possible (traction, numbers)
4. De-risk it (team, strategy, contingencies)
5. Make the ask (clear terms, expected returns)
6. End with inspiration (mission-driven)
