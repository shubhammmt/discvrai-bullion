# DISCVR.AI - 5 Slide Sharp Pitch Deck Structure

## Slide 1: Vision/Title
**Title**: DISCVR.AI: India's Simple Portfolio Holder
**Subtitle**: Intelligent Financial Aggregation for Modern India
**Key Message**: "Your entire financial life, unified in one intelligent platform"

## Slide 2: Problem + Data Insights
**Title**: The Scattered Financial Life Problem

### Key Data Points (from PITCH.md):
- **14 Crore** Indians have Demat accounts (growing 28% YoY)
- **13 Crore** MF investors with ₹61L Cr AUM
- **6.5 Crore** active SIP accounts
- **Average Indian investor**: 3-5 different financial accounts across platforms

### Sharp Insights:
- Multi-account chaos: Users juggling 3-5 brokers, 2-3 MF platforms, banks, insurance
- No single view of portfolio performance, tax implications, or goal tracking
- Manual reconciliation leads to poor financial decisions
- **75% of investors** can't answer "Am I on track for my goals?" (Market research insight)

### Pain Points:
- Portfolio scattered across platforms
- No unified performance tracking
- Tax complexity during filing
- Goal-based planning impossible

## Slide 3: Solution
**Title**: Universal Financial Aggregation + AI Intelligence

### Core Value Props:
1. **Universal Aggregation**: 
   - Connect all accounts: Stocks, MF, Insurance, Bank, Crypto
   - Real-time portfolio sync across 50+ platforms
   - Account Aggregator (AA) framework + API integrations

2. **Intelligent Insights**:
   - AI-powered portfolio analysis
   - Goal-based planning and tracking
   - Tax optimization recommendations
   - Risk assessment and rebalancing alerts

3. **Seamless Action**:
   - Execute trades across platforms
   - Automated rebalancing
   - Goal-based SIP management

### Differentiators:
- **Breadth**: 50+ integrations vs competitors' 10-15
- **Intelligence**: AI-driven insights, not just aggregation
- **Mass Market**: ₹3,800/year vs ₹25,000+ premium solutions
