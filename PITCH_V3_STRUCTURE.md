## Slide 4: Solution Architecture
**Title**: Universal Financial Aggregation + AI Intelligence

### Core Value Props Addressing Each Cohort:

#### 1. **Universal Aggregation Engine**
- **For Basic Users**: Simple portfolio sync across 2-3 platforms
- **For Multi-Platform Users**: Advanced sync across 50+ integrations
- **Technology**: Account Aggregator (AA) framework + API integrations
- **Differentiator**: 50+ integrations vs competitors' 10-15

#### 2. **Intelligent Insights Layer**
- **For Power Users**: Advanced analytics, tax optimization, risk assessment
- **For Portfolio Seekers**: AI-powered goal tracking, performance analysis
- **Technology**: GenAI + ML platform for personalized recommendations
- **Differentiator**: AI-driven insights, not just aggregation

#### 3. **Seamless Action Platform**
- **Cross-Platform Trading**: Execute across multiple brokers
- **Automated Rebalancing**: Based on goals and risk tolerance
- **Goal-Based SIP Management**: Smart automation
- **Differentiator**: Action-oriented, not just tracking

### Market Positioning:
**"Democratizing Wealth Management"** - Institutional-grade analytics at mass-market pricing
