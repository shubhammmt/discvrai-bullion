## Key Investor Risks & Mitigation

### 1. **Execution Risk - CAC Targets**
**Risk**: Our ₹500-600 CAC target is 65% below industry average (₹1,450)
**Mitigation**: 
- Validated viral channels: Referral (₹0-30), Influencer (₹50-150)
- Account Aggregator reduces user acquisition friction (80M+ already comfortable sharing data)
- Strong product-market fit shown in market research (78% face portfolio fragmentation)

### 2. **Regulatory Risk - AI Financial Advice**
**Risk**: SEBI regulations on AI-powered financial recommendations
**Mitigation**:
- Phase 1: Portfolio aggregation (no license needed)
- Phase 2: SEBI IA registration (₹10L deposit) for personalized advice
- Recent SEBI guidelines (Dec 2024) provide clear framework for AI usage

### 3. **Competition Risk - Big Tech Entry**
**Risk**: Google Pay, PhonePe, or banks launching similar platforms
**Mitigation**:
- First-mover advantage in comprehensive aggregation + AI layer
- Deep partnerships with 4 key execution platforms (Smallcase, FMP, CMOTS, Tarakki)
- Focus on AI intelligence vs basic aggregation

### 4. **Technology Risk - Account Aggregator Adoption**
**Risk**: Slow AA adoption or technical challenges
**Mitigation**:
- AA already has 80M+ users and 1059% growth
- Multi-source aggregation: AA + email parsing + manual upload
- Regulatory backing reduces platform risk

### 5. **Unit Economics Risk - Revenue Assumptions**
**Risk**: ₹3,800 ARPU target may be aggressive for mass market
**Mitigation**:
- Tiered pricing: Basic (free), Premium (₹799/month), Power (₹1,499/month)
- Multiple revenue streams: Subscription + transaction commissions + intelligence services
- Conservative projections: 2-5% paid conversion rate

### 6. **Scale Risk - Technical Infrastructure**
**Risk**: Platform performance issues at scale (1M+ users)
**Mitigation**:
- Team has experience scaling to 100M+ users
- Cloud-native architecture from day 1
- 5% budget allocated to infrastructure scaling

### 7. **Market Risk - Economic Downturn**
**Risk**: Economic slowdown reducing user spending on financial services
**Mitigation**:
- Economic uncertainty increases need for portfolio optimization
- Focus on cost-saving insights vs new investments
- Freemium model maintains user base during downturns

### 8. **Partnership Risk - Dependency on External Platforms**
**Risk**: Partner platforms changing terms or restricting access
**Mitigation**:
- Diversified partnership portfolio (4 core partners)
- Direct integrations being developed as backup
- Focus on being aggregator, not dependent on single platform
