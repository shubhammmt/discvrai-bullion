# DISCVR.AI - Investor Deck Format

## Slide Structure

### 1. Title Slide
- Company name: Discvr.ai
- Logo
- Founder & CEO: Shubham Srivastava

### 2. Business Overview
- Quick tagline/slogan
- What business are you in
- What problem do you solve
- Why does the problem exist
- What advantages do you have
- Why should I or anyone care

### 3. Team + Advisors
- Shubham Srivastava (CEO)
- Team members
- Advisors

### 4. Market Opportunity
- How big is the opportunity
- Quantified market size
- Bottom-up analysis: Start from specific users who would use the product, then scale up to total addressable market

### 5. Product/Service/Cause
- Name
- Screen captures
- Demo

### 6. Business Model
- How will you make money
- GTM (Go-to-Market)
- How will you reach customers
- What channels and verticals
- What traction do you have
- How will you build momentum

### 7. Strategic Partnerships
- Partnerships
- Customers
- Letters of Intent (LOI)

### 8. Competition
- Who are they
- How are you different/better

### 9. Barriers to Entry
- What keeps others out
- IP (Intellectual Property)
- How defensible
- Team

### 10. Financial Overview
- Investments to date
- Simple top-line 5-year projection
- Identify break-even and profitability

### 11. Use of Proceeds
- Past
- Now
- Future

### 12. Request of Funds
- Amount seeking
- Terms

### 13. Contact and Close
- Contact information
- Closing statement
