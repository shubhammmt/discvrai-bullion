# Deep Industries — AI-Enabled Digital Transformation Proposal

## Context

### Who is Deep Industries?
- **Business**: Oil & gas field services (gas compression, drilling, workover, dehydration, integrated project management)
- **Scale**: ₹3,050 Cr+ order book; ₹809 Cr TTM revenue; 99.6% operational efficiency
- **Key assets**: 40 wells under PEC (₹1,402 Cr contract), Prabha DP2 barge, 12 workover + 6 drilling rigs, fleet across Mexico/Africa/Argentina
- **Recent event**: Mori #5 gas leakage incident — contained in 5 days; 2–3 month PEC ramp delay
- **Audience**: Promoters and leadership who are confused about AI value. They need business outcomes, not tech jargon.

### Design Principles
- **Tone**: Confident, understanding, not pushy. We know their business.
- **Language**: Business outcomes only. No "AI agents," "machine learning," "IoT" — use "systems that predict," "monitoring," "visibility," "real-time dashboards."
- **Visual**: Clean, professional. Oil & gas / energy aesthetic. Deep blue or teal accent.
