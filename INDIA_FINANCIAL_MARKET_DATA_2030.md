## Key Market Insights

### Growth Drivers
1. **Digital Adoption**: Account Aggregator framework enabling 1000% YoY growth
2. **Wealth Creation**: Rising disposable income driving AUM growth across categories
3. **Financial Inclusion**: Government initiatives expanding insurance and banking access
4. **Generational Shift**: Millennials and Gen-Z driving digital-first financial behavior

### Market Opportunity
- **Total Financial Assets**: ₹2000+ Lakh crore market by 2030
- **Mass Market Focus**: 25m SAM target aligns with 15m-24m SOM projection
- **Multi-Product Opportunity**: Average user holds 3-4 financial products
- **Account Aggregation**: Critical infrastructure enabling unified financial management

### Strategic Positioning
- Target the **Mass Market segment** with 25m SAM by 2030
- Focus on **digital-native users** with regular portfolio engagement
- Leverage **Account Aggregator** framework for comprehensive data access
- Build for **multi-product complexity** - average user juggles 3-4 financial accounts
