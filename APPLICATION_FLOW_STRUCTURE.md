# Application Flow Structure

## Main Navigation Structure
**Feed | Market | Stocks | US Stocks | Mutual Funds | IPOs | Crypto | Organize | Portfolio**

## Detailed Page Breakdown

### 1. Feed (`/feed`)
**Purpose:** Personalized user dashboard and starting point
- Personalized content based on user's watchlist
- User-specific market movers 
- Personalized news and insights
- Quick access to all major sections

### 2. Market (`/market`) 
**Purpose:** General market overview and indices
- General market overview (Sensex, Nifty movements)
- Market-wide top gainers/losers
- General market news
- Overall market sentiment and statistics
- Market breadth indicators

### 3. Asset Class Pages (Consistent Structure)

#### 3.1 Stocks (`/stocks`) - Indian Market
- Hero section with Indian market key metrics
- AI Discovery themes (backend-enabled)
- Search section (AI + conventional filters)
- Trending Indian stocks
- All Indian stocks with advanced filtering
- Individual discovery theme pages (`/stocks/discovery/growth-stocks`)

#### 3.2 US Stocks (`/us-stocks`) - US Market  
- Hero section with US market key metrics
- AI Discovery themes for US market
- Search section (AI + conventional filters)
- Trending US stocks
- All US stocks with advanced filtering
- Individual discovery theme pages (`/us-stocks/discovery/tech-stocks`)

#### 3.3 Mutual Funds (`/mutual-funds`)
- Hero section with mutual fund market overview
- AI Discovery themes (sector funds, theme-based funds, etc.)
- Search section (AI + conventional filters)
- Trending mutual funds
- All mutual funds with advanced filtering
- Individual discovery theme pages (`/mutual-funds/discovery/equity-funds`)

#### 3.4 IPOs (`/ipos`)
- Hero section with upcoming IPO calendar
- AI Discovery themes (upcoming IPOs, recent listings, etc.)
- Search section (AI + conventional filters)
- Current/upcoming IPOs
- All IPO listings with filtering
- Individual discovery theme pages (`/ipos/discovery/mainboard-ipos`)

#### 3.5 Crypto (`/crypto`)
- Hero section with crypto market overview
- AI Discovery themes (DeFi, Layer-1, etc.)
- Search section (AI + conventional filters)
- Trending cryptocurrencies
- All crypto assets with filtering
- Individual discovery theme pages (`/crypto/discovery/defi-tokens`)

### 4. Organize (`/organize`)
**Purpose:** User's personal financial organization hub
- Watchlist management across all asset classes
- Alerts summary and configuration
- User preferences and settings
- Saved searches and discoveries
- Personal notes and research

### 5. Portfolio (`/portfolio`)
**Purpose:** Portfolio management and tracking
- Portfolio summary and performance
- Account connector and integrations
- Asset allocation analysis
- Performance tracking and analytics
- Rebalancing suggestions

## Key Design Principles

### Consistent Asset Class Structure
Each asset class page follows the same pattern:
1. **Hero Section** - Key metrics and market snapshot
2. **AI Discovery** - Backend-enabled thematic discoveries
3. **Search Section** - Unified AI and filter-based search
4. **Trending/Popular** - What's hot in this asset class
5. **All Items** - Complete listing with advanced filtering
6. **Discovery Pages** - Individual theme exploration

### Discovery System
- **AI-Powered:** Backend agents enable discoveries based on core logic
- **Thematic:** Organized around investment themes and strategies
- **Cross-Asset:** Similar themes across different asset classes
- **Personalized:** Tailored to user preferences and behavior

### Navigation Flow Examples
- **Discovery Flow:** Feed → Stocks → Discovery Theme → Individual Stock
- **Search Flow:** Feed → Search → Results → Asset Detail
- **Market Flow:** Feed → Market → Asset Class → Individual Asset
- **Organization Flow:** Feed → Organize → Watchlist → Asset Detail

### Main Landing Page
**Feed** serves as the personalized starting point where users get a dashboard view of their interests and can navigate to any section of the application.

## Technical Implementation Notes
- All asset class pages share common components and patterns
- Discovery themes are dynamically generated by backend AI
- Search functionality combines AI-powered and traditional filtering
- Navigation maintains context across different asset classes
- Responsive design ensures consistent experience across devices
